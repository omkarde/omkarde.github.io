PROJECTS UNDERTAKING IN ASSOCIATION WITH THE GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS

We have been working in close association with various departments of State and Central Government since past many years. Following are some of the projects mentioned :

Name Of Department Project Details In Brief
Ministry of Environment & Forest (MoEF) through BAIF, Pune. National environmental awareness Campaign (NEAC) From 1998 to onward on issues like biodiversity conservation, water elixir of life, SWM, etc.
Ministry of Human Resources & Development (MoHRD), New Delhi. (1) Environment Orientation to School Education (EOSE) for students from the schools of Nagpur.
(2) Implementation of a project of Education in Human Values(EHV) amongst schools of Nagpur city.
Ministry of Environment & Forests (MoEF) through state Forest Govt. of Maharashtra. Evaluation of plantation & other entry Activities in Nagpur, Bhandara & Chandrapur district under National Afforestation programmes (NAP) of NAEB through Forest Development Agency (FDA) project villages.
Water Supply & Sanitation department (A project of World Bank project) for pure water sanitation & women empowerment. JALSWARAJ project in progress in 15 Gram Panchayats of Bhandara district & 10 Gram panchayats of Nagpur district. NiViM is supporting organization (SO) for capacity building of villages by the participation of people.
